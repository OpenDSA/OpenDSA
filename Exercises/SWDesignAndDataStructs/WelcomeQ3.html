In-person CS 2114 students must register for both a Lecture and a Lab. Registering for a Lab section means that your WebCAT profile will be configured to allow you to electronically submit labs and assignments to that section.    The course team understands that students may need to make adjustments to their class schedule during the first couple weeks of the semester.   If a student switches Lab sections (registers for a different Lab section than they were previously assigned to in Web-CAT) the student must:  

contact instructors immediately, either via email or a piazza post, advising them of the details of the change in lab sections
  • do nothing
  • wait until after the submission deadline to contact instructors
